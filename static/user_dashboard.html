Smart Farming Recommendations

Irrigation Tips

  • • Water crops early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation
  • • Use drip irrigation for 30-50% water savings
  • • Monitor soil moisture before watering

Crop Management

  • • Rotate crops to improve soil health
  • • Apply organic fertilizers for sustainable growth
  • • Regular pest monitoring reduces yield loss

Yield Optimization

  • • Maintain soil pH between 6.0-7.5 for most crops
  • • Ensure 6-8 hours of sunlight for optimal photosynthesis
  • • Use AI predictions to plan planting schedule

Disease Prevention

  • • Inspect plants weekly for early disease signs
  • • Maintain proper plant spacing for air circulation
  • • Use resistant crop varieties when available
